Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add items to my shopping list?

You can add items by typing them in the text field or by using the voice input button to speak your items.

Does TellmeList work without an internet connection?

Yes! TellmeList is designed to work completely offline. All your data is stored locally on your device.

Is my voice data stored or sent to servers?

No. Voice recognition is processed entirely on your device using Apple's built-in speech recognition. Voice data is converted to text locally and immediately discarded.

How do I delete my shopping lists?

You can delete individual items by swiping left, or delete entire lists using the delete function. To remove all app data, simply delete the app from your iPhone.

Is TellmeList free?

Yes, TellmeList is completely free to use with no hidden costs or in-app purchases.
